Scrambled Eggs with Veggies

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup chopped bell pepper
  • ¼ cup chopped onion
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp butter or oil

Steps:

  1. Heat butter or oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onions and bell peppers until soft.
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl and add salt and pepper.
  4. Pour the eggs into the pan with the veggies.
  5. Stir gently until eggs are cooked but still soft.
  6. Serve warm.
