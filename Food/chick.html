Chicken Wrap

Ingredients:

  • 1 tortilla or flatbread
  • ½ cup cooked chicken, shredded
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise or yogurt
  • ⅛ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper

Steps:

  1. Lay the tortilla flat on a clean surface.
  2. Spread mayonnaise or yogurt evenly over the tortilla.
  3. Place lettuce and tomato slices on top.
  4. Add shredded chicken.
  5. Sprinkle salt and pepper over the filling.
  6. Roll the tortilla tightly.
  7. Cut in half and serve.
