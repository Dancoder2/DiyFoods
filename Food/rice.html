Vegetable Stir-Fry with Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • ½ cup peas
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add carrots, bell pepper, and peas.
  3. Stir-fry for 5–7 minutes until vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Add cooked rice and mix everything together.
  6. Serve hot.
